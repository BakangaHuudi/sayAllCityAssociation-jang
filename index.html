WELCOME TO SAY ALL CITY OFFICIAL PAGE


Say All City is an association formed by some able youth of jang aimed at curtailing the slow development of jang electoral area. It involvs a lot opinion

leaders who bring their vision and abilites together to dicuss issues that will bring devlopment to jang electoral area. The aim of this webpage is to help

communicate to our able and caring brothers who are from jang electorial area but resident in places like Wa, Accra and other towns and cities accross the

country and the world at large to come to our aid. Individuals and organisations who interested in providing a helping hand are welcomed. Though association

is NON-POLITICAL, any help from any political party is highly welcomed. any individual, organisation or politcal party who wishes to offer a helping hand or

know more about the association should click contcat below:
